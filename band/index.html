We Love BLACKPINK

BlackPink (Korean: 블랙핑크; Romaja: Beullaekpingkeu; often stylized as BLɅϽKPIИK) is a South Korean girl group formed and managed by YG Entertainment. The group consists of 4 members including Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. Blackpink officially debuted with their first single album titled "Square One", consisting of two songs "Boombayah" and "Whistle". The two subsequently entered Billboard's World Digital Songs chart and Korea's Gaon digital music chart.
